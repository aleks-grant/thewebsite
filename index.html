About Me

I'm a Computer Science major currently studying at Fairleigh Dickinson University in Madison, New Jersey. In addition to my studies, I'm also a member of the lacrosse team, where I enjoy competing and staying active.

My programming skills are strongest in C++ and Python. I'm also suffiecent in C# and JavaScript, having worked on various projects and assignments in these languages.

Outside of academics, I'm a passionate hockey fan, supporting the New York Rangers. Whether it's watching games or following the latest news, hockey is a big part of my life.

One of my other passions is music. I have a deep appreciation for rock/alternative music and love exploring albums. Music is a huge source of inspiration for me.
